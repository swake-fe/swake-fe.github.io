Sarah is a horticulturist with over 10 years of experience. She holds her Bachelor's Degree in Biology from Iowa State University and received her Master's Degree in Horticulture from Oregon State University. She currently works full-time in Chicago with an urban design firm and consults local garden retail stores and urban farms throughout Chicagoland. Apart from tending to her home garden, Sarah enjoys travel, romance novels and kayaking.
